About Us

As a specialist bespoke jeweller, we do things a little differently. Our streamlined approach lets us focus solely on the jewellery and give you a choice; bigger or better? We trim everything that causes you to pay more from the High Street to give you better diamonds and value. There is no in-store pressure, just a wonderfully simple service that gives you more for your money and allows you to spend more on truly important experiences. A lifelong piece deserves to be uniquely you, tailored to your desires. You deserve a piece that cannot be found elsewhere, and you can get that through AKA Bespoke.
